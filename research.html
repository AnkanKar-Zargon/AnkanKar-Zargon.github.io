Journey into Discovery: My Research Portfolio

My research interests cover varied areas of computer science, statistics and mathematics. I mainly work on Causal Graphical and Probabilistic Models, Statistical Learning Theory and Randomized Algorithms. Recently, I have worked on constructing an automated stock market trading model based on Reinforcement Learning and Distribution Theory. As a researcher at the previous company I worked in the R&D of AI division, I worked on generating synthetic data from causal models, time-varying causal analytics, and causal model generation from datasets.
