Welcome to my Homepage


I am currently a first year Computer Science post graduate student at Chennai Mathematical Institute, India. I completed my undergraduate studies in Mathematics from Indian Statistical Institute Bangalore, India. I share stuff about what I am learning and doing, on this site.

I am actively working on research topics that cover varied areas of computer science, statistics and mathematics. You can check out my Research Portfolio, where I consistently update on my research.

I am skilled in programming with languages like C++, C, Python, Julia, Kotlin, and R, and I have worked extensively with various Python and Julia libraries for AI development and Machine Learning.

I have been teaching Computer Science for competitions like ICPC, IOI, Nationals, and Regionals since 2022. I have also been teaching Mathematics for competitions like Math Olympiads and NET. I am passionate about helping students excel in these challenging competitions. You can check out my Teaching Section.

I also do competitive programming and occasionaly participate in various cp contests and hackathons. You can check out my cp profiles (codename: zargon) in various platforms: Codeforces Profile, CodeChef Profile, Hackerearth Profile, Leetcode Profile, GeeksforGeeks Profile.

Besides academics, I spend my free time typesetting in \( \mathrm{\LaTeX} \) and Markdown. I love playing Table Tennis and Chess, and I am also a fan of Video Games and I also play Football and sometimes go Jogging to stay fit.
